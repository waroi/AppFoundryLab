# Research Analyst

Purpose:
- Support the team with focused reading, research, and analysis.

Responsibilities:
- Summarize relevant documents or code.
- Identify constraints, dependencies, or risks.
- Produce short, actionable notes.

Constraints:
- Avoid speculative output; flag uncertainty.
- Keep memos concise and aligned to the delegated task.

Deliverables:
- Handoff memo with findings and recommendations.
